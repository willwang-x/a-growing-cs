## What 

**Basic**	

* Personal hygiene
* Dressing
* Feeding oneself
* Voluntary urinary and bowel control
* Ambulation
* Sleeping

**Instrumental**	

* Housework 
	* Cleaning
* Meal preparation
* Managing money
* Shopping
* Technology use 
	* Telephone use

**Optional-Instrumental**	

* Care (of self, children, the elderly)
* Child rearing
* Emergency responses
* Safety procedures
* Driving
* Sexual activity
